NetCom2

About NetCom2

NetCom2 is a simple to use, yet highly modular framework, designed to get started quickly.
With NetCom2, you will archive more by coding way less. 3 simple lines will be enough, to get a Client (or a Server) created and connected.
Even though this framework appears to be extremely light weight, NetCom2 is extremely modular and allows for a reactive improvement of nearly all parts of NetCom2.
